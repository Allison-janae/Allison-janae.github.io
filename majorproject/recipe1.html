Cheese Fondue

Warm, cheesy, savory dip perfect for bread, veggies, and more.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ pound shredded Swiss cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Gruyère cheese
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 loaf French bread, cubed

Directions

  1. Heat fondue pot over medium-low.
  2. Bring wine to a gentle simmer.
  3. Stir in flour and nutmeg until smooth.
  4. Add cheeses slowly, melting fully between additions.
  5. Season with salt and serve with French bread.
  6. Enjoy!
