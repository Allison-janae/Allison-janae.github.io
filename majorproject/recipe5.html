Coconut Shrimp

Crunchy and flavorful coconut shrimp, perfect as an appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour or whole wheat flour (spooned & leveled)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 3/4 cup Panko bread crumbs
  • 1 cup sweetened shredded coconut
  • 1 lb raw large shrimp, peeled and deveined, tails attached
  • 3–4 tbsp vegetable oil or coconut oil
  • Optional: 1 tbsp chopped cilantro for garnish

Directions

  1. Thaw shrimp if frozen and pat dry.
  2. Set up breading station: flour, beaten eggs, and Panko + coconut mixture in separate bowls.
  3. Coat each shrimp in flour, then egg, then Panko + coconut.
  4. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat, enough to coat the bottom.
  5. Fry shrimp 2–3 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through. Drain on paper towels.
  6. Optional: Sprinkle with chopped cilantro before serving. Serve with dipping sauce if desired.
  7. Freezing tip: Fried coconut shrimp can be frozen up to 2 months. Reheat at 350°F for 10 minutes.
