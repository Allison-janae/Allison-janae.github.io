Nashville Hot Chicken Dip

Spicy, creamy, flavorful — the perfect party dip.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz reduced-fat cream cheese, softened
  • ⅓ cup fat-free sour cream
  • ⅓ cup nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp hot sauce
  • 1 tbsp dill pickle juice
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tbsp light butter
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp chili powder
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 oz shredded mozzarella (2% milk)
  • 8 oz cooked shredded chicken breast
  • ¼ cup chopped dill pickles

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix cream cheese, sour cream, yogurt, hot sauce, pickle juice, and honey.
  3. Melt butter, then stir in brown sugar, cayenne, paprika, chili powder, garlic powder, and salt.
  4. Combine spiced butter with cheese mixture, then stir in mozzarella and chicken.
  5. Spread mixture in a 9” pie dish and bake 25–30 minutes.
  6. Top with chopped pickles and serve warm.
