Chicken Avocado Roll-ups

Fresh Chicken Roll-ups

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked chicken breast, diced or shredded
  • 1 avocado, pitted and diced
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese of choice
  • 1/4 cup diced tomato
  • 2 tablespoons onion, minced (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons cilantro, minced
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 – 10" flour tortillas, or 3 medium tortillas

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine chicken, avocado, cheese, tomato, onion, cilantro, sour cream, and lime juice. Season with salt and pepper, stirring until well mixed and avocado is slightly mashed.
  2. Spread the mixture evenly over the tortillas and roll them up tightly. Slice as desired and serve immediately, or refrigerate until ready to serve. Enjoy hot or cold!
