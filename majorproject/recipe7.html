Fruit Dip

Sweet, light, and creamy dip perfect for fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 (7 oz) jar marshmallow fluff
  • 1 (8 oz) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • Splash of maraschino cherry juice

Directions

  1. Place all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Blend with a hand mixer until smooth and creamy.
  3. Refrigerate for 1–2 hours before serving.
  4. Serve chilled with fresh fruit.
