Fruit Dip

Sweet, light & creamy dip, perfect for fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 (7 ounce) jar marshmallow fluff
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • Splash of maraschino cherry juice

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine marshmallow fluff, cream cheese, vanilla yogurt, and cherry juice.
  2. Use a hand mixer or stand mixer to blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Transfer to a serving bowl and refrigerate for 1–2 hours before serving.
  4. Serve with fresh fruit for dipping.
