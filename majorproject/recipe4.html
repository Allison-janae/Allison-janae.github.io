Philly Cheesesteak Sliders

Delicious, cheesy, savory handheld sliders.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 large green pepper, thinly sliced
  • ½ lb fresh shaved steak
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 (12-pack) sweet rolls
  • 6 slices provolone cheese
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • ½ tsp Worcestershire sauce (extra)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add onions and peppers and cook until softened.
  3. Add shaved steak, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until steak is no longer red.
  4. Slice sweet rolls in half horizontally, keeping them connected. Place bottom half on a baking sheet.
  5. Spread steak mixture evenly over the rolls. Top with provolone slices and the top half of the rolls.
  6. Mix melted butter, thyme, and remaining Worcestershire sauce. Brush over the rolls.
  7. Bake 17–20 minutes or until golden brown and cheese is melted. Serve warm.
