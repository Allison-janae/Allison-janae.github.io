Greek Feta Dip

Creamy and refreshing dip, perfect for bagels, pita, or veggies.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz feta cheese, drained
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes
  • Diced cucumbers and diced tomatoes for garnish

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine feta, Greek yogurt, cream cheese, garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, and olive oil.
  2. Use a hand mixer or stand mixer to blend until light and fluffy.
  3. Transfer to a serving bowl.
  4. Garnish with a drizzle of olive oil, fresh dill, red pepper flakes, diced cucumbers, and diced tomatoes.
  5. Serve with bagels, pita bread, or chips.
