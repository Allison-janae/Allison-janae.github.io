Greek Feta Dip

Creamy and refreshing Greek feta dip, perfect with pita or veggies.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces feta cheese, drained
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • ½ cup cream cheese (4 ounces), softened
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • Pinch of red pepper flakes
  • Diced cucumbers and diced tomatoes for garnish

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine feta, Greek yogurt, cream cheese, garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, and olive oil.
  2. Using a hand mixer or stand mixer, mix until light and fluffy. Transfer to a serving bowl.
  3. Garnish with olive oil, fresh dill, red pepper flakes, diced cucumbers, and tomatoes.
  4. Serve with pita, bagels, or chips.
