Bacon Wrapped Scallops

Savory, crispy, sweet-and-salty perfection.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs large sea scallops, patted dry
  • 1 lb bacon slices, cut in half
  • ¼ cup maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Cooking spray

Directions

  1. Preheat broiler and coat a sheet pan with cooking spray.
  2. Wrap each scallop in a half slice of bacon and secure with a toothpick. Place in a single layer on the pan.
  3. Mix maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Brush half the mixture over the scallops.
  5. Broil 10–15 minutes, brushing with remaining sauce halfway through.
  6. Sprinkle with parsley and serve warm.
