Bacon Wrapped Scallops

Savory Crispy Scallops

Ingredients

  • Bacon Scallop Ingredients
    • 2 pounds large sea scallops patted dry
    • ▢ 1 pound bacon slices cut in half crosswise
    • ▢ 1/4 cup maple syrup
    • ▢ 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • ▢ 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    • ▢ salt and pepper to taste
    • ▢ 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
    • ▢ cooking spray

Directions

  1. heat fondu pot
  2. Prepare the Scallops:
    1. Preheat the broiler. Coat a sheet pan with cooking spray.
    2. Wrap a slice of bacon around each scallop and secure with a toothpick. Arrange the scallops in a single layer on the baking pan.
    3. In a small bowl, mix together the maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Brush half the mixture over the top of each of the scallops.
    4. Broil for 10-15 minutes, or until bacon is crisp and scallops are cooked through. Brush the remaining sauce over the scallops halfway through the cook time.
    5. Sprinkle with parsley, then serve.
