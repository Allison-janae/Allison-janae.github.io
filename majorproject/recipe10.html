Cheese Ball Bites

Cheesy, spreadable bites

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces softened cream cheese (1.5 blocks)
  • 1 tbsp ranch seasoning
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup bacon bits
  • 1 tbsp fresh chopped parsley
  • 30 pretzel sticks

Directions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine softened cream cheese, ranch seasoning, and shredded cheddar cheese. Mix until smooth.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat. Roll the cream cheese mixture into 1-inch balls and place them on the prepared sheet.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine bacon bits and parsley. Roll each cheese ball in the mixture until fully coated, then return to the baking sheet.
  4. Insert a pretzel stick into the center of each cheese ball before serving.
